Website Summary

The website I want to make is a online recipe book/archive that is simple and easily searchable. No fluff, no stories, just recipes.
All the content for this site will be user submitted. Users can submit their favorite recipes so that all users of the site can easily access and enjoy them.

As said by chef and internet personality Josh Scherer, We all gotta eat, and we're all gonna die
Everyone needs to eat but it's sometimes hard to figure out what you want to make.

If this site can help people easily find recipes, who wouldn't want to use it?

"There's already so many websites for recipes already, why make another?"
To put it plainly: recipe website's suck. There full of ads, unnecessary stories, hostile UI, and just bloat in general.
I want to make something simple and easy to use without the excess.
I would like users to submit their recipes to my website, so that others can find them and make them.

The goal of this site is to make finding and keeping recipes simpler and more accessible.
