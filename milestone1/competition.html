Competition

  • Pinterest:

      - Likes: Use of photos, clean/simple UI and color palette (red and white), rounded edges, search bar

      - Dislikes: Too many photos-- would prefer one post at a time; overwhelmed with options

      - How I Can Make It Better: Have posts show 1-3 at a time, depending on what device you are viewing it on

  • Allrecipes:

      - Likes: Recipe cards, ability to save recipes, search tags

      - Dislikes: Too much clutter, looks like a website for a newspaper, in your face/corporate style, too colorful, overwhelming UI

      - How I Can Make It Better: Add a cleaner, more simple UI

  • Food Network:

      - Likes: Simple design and color palette, advanced search options, saves page for quick access to recipes

      - Dislikes: trying to be a news website, too many photos all at once

      - How I Can Make It Better: Show categories/search bar first before showing recipes
